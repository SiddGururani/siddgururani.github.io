This is my ongoing master's thesis at Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. This project combines elements of Computational Musicology and MIR. A preliminary thesis report is available HERE.

Rhythm Extraction

This is a project I worked on during my summer internship at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) under the guidance of Professor Pascale Fung. A project report for this is available HERE.

The work was carried out along with another summer intern, Arjun Rao. The workload was shared equally for the onset detection algorithm section of the project, following which Arjun took leave and I took the project forward.

The internship duration was 10 weeks.
